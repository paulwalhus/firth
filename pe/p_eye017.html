In the Public Eye

Trauma at the National Film Theatre
London, August 22, 2004


The British Film Institute held a special screening of Trauma at the National Film Theatre, which was followed by a conversation with Colin Firth and Marc Evans that lasted nearly an hour. Colin took questions from audience members and a crack team of Drool reporters had the experience of a lifetime.
