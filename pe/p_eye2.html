British Academy Film Awards
24 February 2002


Colin Firth and his wife Livia arrive for the Orange British Academy Film Awards at the Odeon cinema in London's Leicester Square. (right)

Nominated for Best Supporting Actor for Bridget Jones's Diary, the award was won by Jim Broadbent for his role in Moulin Rouge.

A complete listing of the winners can be found at  Bafta's website.

 

 
Shown below in photos exclusive to The Bucket, Colin is headed to the Grosvenor House's ballroom for the post-awards dinner party.
