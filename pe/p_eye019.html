The Woodsman at The Times bfi London Film Festival
London, October 30, 2004



Colin and his wife attended The Times bfi London Film Festival's premiere screening of The Woodsman at the Odeon West End on Saturday, October 30.

Going over to the barricades, Colin signed autographs for fans.

The Woodsman stars Colin's Where the Truth Lies' co-star Kevin Bacon, who also executive produced the film, and his wife Kyra Sedgwick. Director Nicole Kassell's first feature film has already received much acclaim from critics following its Toronto bow as well as Oscar buzz for Bacon, who plays a convicted pedophile who returns home and attempts to reenter society and create a new life for himself, while struggling to deal with his past.
