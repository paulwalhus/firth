Featured — Summer 2026

Disclosure Day

Steven Spielberg’s first original science-fiction film in decades. Set at the moment humanity is told that extraterrestrial life is real — triggering global panic, political collapse, and a reckoning with everything we believed about our place in the universe.

A teaser trailer released in December 2025 generated immediate frenzy, with glimpses of worldwide chaos, shadowy government procedures, and characters in states of extreme distress. First-look images released in January 2026 include Colin strapped to a chair in what appears to be a government facility.

Josh O’Connor plays a whistleblower advocating for total transparency. Emily Blunt and Colin Firth previously appeared together in Mary Poppins Returns (2018). Filmed in New York, New Jersey and Atlanta from February to May 2025.

June 12, 2026 • US Cinema

Director: Steven Spielberg
Screenplay: David Koepp
Stars: Emily Blunt, Josh O’Connor, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell, Colin Firth

Young Sherlock

Prime Video

An action-packed origin story following a young Sherlock Holmes (Hero Fiennes Tiffin) on his first ever case. Colin plays Sir Bucephalus Hodge. Created and directed by Guy Ritchie. 8 episodes, all available from launch. Variety: “a perfect origin story.”

Also stars: Donal Finn, Joseph Fiennes, Natascha McElhone, Max Irons.

2026 • Currently Filming, Berlin

Berlin Noir

Apple TV+

Adaptation of Philip Kerr’s 14-book Bernie Gunther detective series, set in 1928 Berlin. Colin plays Paul Lohser — a meticulous, prickly murder detective who becomes Bernie’s unlikely partner and mentor.

Stars: Jack Lowden as Bernie Gunther
Written by: Peter Straughan (who co-wrote Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy with Colin)
Produced by: Bad Wolf & PlayTone (Tom Hanks/Gary Goetzman)

Positioned as Apple’s next major drama series following the acclaimed Slow Horses. Casting confirmed February 2026.

2026 • Post-Production

Kingsman: The Blue Blood

20th Century Studios

The concluding chapter of the Harry Hart / Eggsy trilogy. Director Matthew Vaughn has described the relationship between these two characters as the franchise’s emotional spine, and says The Blue Blood brings their story to a definitive end.

Director: Matthew Vaughn
Stars: Colin Firth, Taron Egerton
Release date: TBA
