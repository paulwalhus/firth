The Story

Synopsis

When the Kingsman headquarters is destroyed and most of the agency killed, Eggsy (Taron Egerton) and Merlin (Mark Strong) are forced to seek out their American counterparts — the Statesman, a spy organisation operating beneath the cover of a Kentucky whisky distillery.

There they discover that Harry Hart / Galahad (Colin Firth) — shot through the eye at the climax of The Secret Service — is alive. The Statesman have kept him in a state of chemically induced amnesia; he now believes himself to be an entomology professor named Jack Daniels and has no memory of Kingsman, his training, or Eggsy.

Together, the combined organisations must confront Poppy Adams (Julianne Moore), a deranged drug kingpin who has poisoned a global supply of recreational narcotics and is holding billions of lives hostage until the US government legalises drugs.

Production

Notes

  • Released September 20, 2017 (UK) and September 22, 2017 (US). Worldwide gross: $410 million.
  • Harry Hart’s survival was kept largely secret in pre-release marketing. Colin’s return was a genuine surprise for audiences.
  • The amnesia storyline gives Colin a rare opportunity to play Harry Hart without his characteristic urbane menace — gentle, confused, and strangely touching.
  • A third Kingsman film, The Blue Blood, is in post-production as of early 2026, with Colin set to return as Harry Hart for the final chapter of the trilogy alongside Taron Egerton.
